Frequently asked questions

How much does the average wedding cost?

The average US wedding costs roughly $33,000, though it varies widely by region and guest count. Catering scales hardest with headcount, so trimming the guest list is the fastest way to cut the total.

What is a standard wedding budget breakdown?

A common split is venue 30%, catering 22%, photo & video 12%, flowers & decor 8%, music 7%, attire & beauty 6%, with the rest across stationery, planning, transportation, ceremony, and a misc buffer.

How much should I keep as a contingency?

Keep about 5% of your total budget untouched as a contingency for surprises and tips. Bridezilla holds that buffer aside automatically and flags any quote that blows past its category.
