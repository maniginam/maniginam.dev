Frequently asked questions

Is Bridezilla a subscription?

No. It is a one-time per-wedding access fee, not a recurring subscription. Your engagement lasts months around one date, so you pay once for the whole run.

What is in the free plan?

The free plan includes the full wedding plan, a budget tracker, and a vendor shortlist. There is no autonomous outreach on the free tier.

What does the paid per-wedding plan add?

For $99–199 per wedding you unlock Bridezilla's working inbox, draft-and-approve vendor outreach, the full timeline, and budget guardrails.

Will Bridezilla ever charge a vendor or pay a deposit?

Never. Bridezilla has no payment ability at all. It tracks numbers on paper; you handle every actual payment yourself.
