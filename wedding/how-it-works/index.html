How Bridezilla works

Quick answer

Bridezilla works like a real wedding planner that lives in its own email inbox. You give it the basics; it builds the plan, researches vendors, drafts every outreach email, and tracks the budget. It never sends, signs, or pays without your explicit one-tap approval.

Plan, draft, approve — in four steps

  1. 1 Tell it the basics

    Date, budget, vibe, guest count, location.

  2. 2 It builds the plan

    Timeline, budget allocation, and a vendor shortlist.

  3. 3 It drafts every email

    Outreach and replies, written for you in its own inbox.

  4. 4 You approve before it sends

    One tap to send, or godzilla in and take the wheel.

Frequently asked questions

Can AI plan my whole wedding?

Bridezilla can build the entire plan, budget, and vendor outreach autonomously. The one thing it never does on its own is send: every email waits for your one-tap approval.

Is it safe to let an AI handle vendor outreach?

Yes — because nothing sends without you. Bridezilla works in its own inbox, identifies clearly as your planning assistant, never impersonates you, never signs a contract, and never moves money.

What is the godzilla override?

At any moment you can take over a thread, edit any draft, pause the agent, or revoke its autonomy for a specific vendor. You are always in control.
