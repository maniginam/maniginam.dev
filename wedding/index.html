Frequently asked questions

What is an AI wedding planner?

An AI wedding planner is software that builds your wedding plan, budget, timeline, and vendor outreach automatically. Bridezilla goes further: it works in its own inbox and drafts every email, then you approve before anything sends.

Does Bridezilla spend my money or sign contracts?

No. Bridezilla tracks your budget on paper and drafts outreach, but it never moves money and never signs anything. Every email needs your one-tap approval, and you can take over any thread at any time.

How is Bridezilla different from a checklist app?

A checklist app gives you tasks; Bridezilla does the tasks. It researches vendors, drafts the emails, chases replies, and keeps the budget honest — you just approve.

Is there a free version?

Yes. The free plan gives you the full plan, a budget tracker, and a vendor shortlist. The per-wedding paid plan adds Bridezilla's working inbox with draft-and-approve outreach.
