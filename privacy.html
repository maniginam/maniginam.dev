Who We Are

ManiGinaM LLC is a woman-owned software craftsmanship and consulting business based in Port Allen, Louisiana, United States. Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to gina@cleancoders.com.

Information We Collect

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

We use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and to keep the site secure and functional. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some features may not work as intended if you do.

LinkedIn Integration

We use the LinkedIn Marketing / Community Management APIs solely to publish our own content (posts and updates) to the maniginam.dev LinkedIn Company Page that we administer. This integration:

  • Publishes content we author to our own Company Page.
  • Uses OAuth access tokens issued to us by LinkedIn, stored securely and never shared with third parties.
  • Does not collect, store, or process the personal data of LinkedIn members, followers, or other users.

Your activity on LinkedIn itself is governed by LinkedIn's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

  • To operate, secure, and improve maniginam.dev.

  • To publish our own marketing content to our LinkedIn Company Page.

Third-Party Services

We rely on the following processors, each with its own privacy policy:

We keep analytics data for as long as it is useful for understanding site performance, and we retain messages you send us only as long as needed to respond and keep reasonable records. We delete or anonymize data when it is no longer needed.
